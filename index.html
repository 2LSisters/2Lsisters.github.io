About
Mornington locals and visitors head to hidden gem 2 Left Sisters tucked away on Watt Road in the peninsula’s industrial area. Named for two sisters who own the café, this drive-through and dine-in eatery is the place to head when the weather turns chilly; sit by the fire inside and join the lively buzz of conversation, or choose to use the drive and stay warm and dry while picking up lunch. Be tempted by breaky plates like a scrambled egg and chorizo burrito with black beans, corn, cheddar and chipotle mayo, toasted in a tortilla with smashed avocado, or indulge in berry bliss pancakes with ice cream, berry coulis and honeycomb. For a healthy lunch, choose a bowl with zucchini and corn fritters, salmon, turmeric hummus and rocket salad, or devour a double cheeseburger and chips.
